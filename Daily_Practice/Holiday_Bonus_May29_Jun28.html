📌 इसे कैसे उपयोग करें — How to Use This Schedule

  1. Every day, first complete the regular W0X daily practice (5 days × 40 min Mon–Fri, weekend mock Sat, challenge Sun).
  2. Then open this bonus page and do the day's 30–40 min extra activity from the Pinki teacher materials.
  3. PDFs are in the "pinki hindi teacher" folder on your computer.
  4. Write answers in a dedicated holiday notebook — don't print unless needed.
  5. For Paper Leftovers: open the same week's HTML file and scroll to any question you skipped.
