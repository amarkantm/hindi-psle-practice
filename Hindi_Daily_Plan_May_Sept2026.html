How to Print Each Day's Practice

1
Click a week link above to open that week's practice file.
2
Press Ctrl+P (or Cmd+P on Mac) and choose A4 paper, Portrait.
3
Print only the days you need — each day is a separate page block.
4
Fold or cut the Answer Key so the child can check after completing.
5
Child writes answers on the printed sheet — builds exam-day handwriting habit.
6
Fill in time tracker + star rating after each session. Track progress!
